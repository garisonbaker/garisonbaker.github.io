Phi Mu Alpha Epsilon Lambda Chapter

Founded in 1898 by Ossian Everett Mills, Phi Mu Alpha is the world's oldest and largest fraternal society in music. Since it's founding in 1950, UGA's Epsilon Lamda chapter of PMA has spread the ideals of Phi Mu Alpha here in Athens. The Epsilon Lambda Chapter seeks to further the Ossian Everett Mills Music Mission in Athens by routinely visiting places in the community to sing and share music. We seek to spread music and fellowship to the community in order to help further advance music in America in accordance to our noble object.
